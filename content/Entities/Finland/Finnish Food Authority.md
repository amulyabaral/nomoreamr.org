# Finnish Food Authority (Ruokavirasto)

- **Country**: [[Finland]]
- **Type**: Government agency
- **Website**: [ruokavirasto.fi](https://www.ruokavirasto.fi/en/)

[[Finland]]'s authority for food safety, animal health, and plant health. Coordinates **FINRES-Vet** for veterinary [[Antimicrobial Resistance (AMR)|AMR]] surveillance.

## Role in AMR Surveillance

- Coordinates [[FINRES-FINRES-Vet|FINRES-Vet]] for veterinary AMR monitoring
- Contributes to surveillance of pathogenic, zoonotic, and indicator bacteria
- Covers industrial food production, meat, fur industry, companion animals, and horses
- Collaborates with [[Fimea]] on veterinary [[Antimicrobial Use (AMU)|AMU]] data
