# Finnish Medicines Agency (Fimea)

- **Country**: [[Finland]]
- **Type**: Government agency
- **Website**: [fimea.fi](https://www.fimea.fi/)

Responsible for monitoring and providing data on antibiotic sales and consumption in both the human and veterinary sectors in [[Finland]].

## Role in AMU Surveillance

- Provides data on human antibiotic sales and consumption for [[FINRES-FINRES-Vet|FINRES]]
- Provides veterinary antibiotic sales data from pharmaceutical wholesalers for [[FINRES-FINRES-Vet|FINRES-Vet]]
- Collects information from wholesalers on antibiotics supplied to hospitals
- Maintains the **Finnish Prescription Register (FPR)** (since 1993) - includes patient demographics, prescribed antibiotics, and dosages
