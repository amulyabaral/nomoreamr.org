# Finnish Institute for Health and Welfare (THL)

- **Country**: [[Finland]]
- **Type**: Government agency
- **Website**: [thl.fi](https://thl.fi/en/)

[[Finland]]'s national public health agency. THL has coordinated human AMR data collection since 1992 through the **FiRe laboratories** network. It compiles, interprets, and publishes annual **Finres reports** on [[Antimicrobial Resistance (AMR)|antimicrobial resistance]].

## Role in AMR Surveillance

- Collects clinical microbiology laboratory data for [[FINRES-FINRES-Vet|FINRES]]
- Maintains and publishes the FiRe laboratories data (collaboration since 1992)
- Operates the **National Infectious Disease Register (NIDR)** - mandatory notification for 40 infectious diseases
- Contributes to animal surveillance of pathogenic, zoonotic, and indicator bacteria
- Provides open-access AMR database: [sampo.thl.fi](https://sampo.thl.fi/pivot/prod/fi/finres/lite/fact_lite)
