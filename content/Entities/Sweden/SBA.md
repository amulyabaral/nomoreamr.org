# Swedish Board of Agriculture (Jordbruksverket)

- **Country**: [[Sweden]]
- **Type**: Government agency
- **Website**: [jordbruksverket.se](https://jordbruksverket.se/)

Contributes to veterinary [[Antimicrobial Use (AMU)|antimicrobial use]] data for [[Sweden]].

## Role in AMU Surveillance

- Contributes to veterinary antibiotic sales and consumption data for [[Swedres-Svarm|Svarm]]
- Implements EU Regulation (EU) 2019/6 on veterinary medicinal products at national level
