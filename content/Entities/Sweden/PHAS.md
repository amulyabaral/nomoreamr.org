# Public Health Agency of Sweden (Folkhälsomyndigheten)

- **Country**: [[Sweden]]
- **Type**: Government agency
- **Website**: [folkhalsomyndigheten.se](https://www.folkhalsomyndigheten.se/)

[[Sweden]]'s national public health agency. Manages the **Swedres** component of [[Swedres-Svarm]], collecting clinical microbiology laboratory data on [[Antimicrobial Resistance (AMR)|AMR]].

## Role in AMR Surveillance

- Collects clinical microbiology laboratory data for [[Swedres-Svarm|Swedres]]
- Manages **SmiNet** (notifiable disease reporting) and **Svebar** (AMR data reporting)
- Automatic reporting of susceptibility test results from clinical laboratories
- Covers invasive and non-invasive isolates from primary and specialist care
- Routine surveillance for ESBL-producing E. coli
