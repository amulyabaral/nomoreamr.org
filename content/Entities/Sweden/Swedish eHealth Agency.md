# Swedish eHealth Agency

- **Country**: [[Sweden]]
- **Type**: Government agency
- **Website**: [ehalsomyndigheten.se](https://www.ehalsomyndigheten.se/)

Manages the pharmaceutical sales statistics database, providing [[Antimicrobial Use (AMU)|AMU]] data for [[Sweden]].

## Role in AMU Surveillance

- Manages the database of pharmaceutical sales statistics
- Provides data on human antibiotic sales and consumption for [[Swedres-Svarm|Swedres]]
- Collects sales data for use in humans, prescriptions to animal owners, and veterinary practices
