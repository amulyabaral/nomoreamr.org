# Swedish Veterinary Agency (SVA)

- **Country**: [[Sweden]]
- **Type**: Research institute
- **Website**: [sva.se](https://www.sva.se/en/)

[[Sweden]]'s national veterinary institute. Manages the **Svarm** component of [[Swedres-Svarm]], monitoring [[Antimicrobial Resistance (AMR)|AMR]] in animals and food.

## Role in AMR Surveillance

- Manages [[Swedres-Svarm|Svarm]] (veterinary component)
- Monitors commensal bacteria in food-producing animals from slaughterhouses
- Routinely collects diagnostic samples from diseased animals
- Contributes to veterinary antibiotic sales and consumption data
