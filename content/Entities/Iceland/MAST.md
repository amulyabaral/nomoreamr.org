# Icelandic Food and Veterinary Authority (MAST)

- **Country**: [[Iceland]]
- **Type**: Government agency
- **Website**: [mast.is](https://www.mast.is/)

[[Iceland]]'s main authority for animal health, food safety, and veterinary medicines.

## Role in AMR Surveillance

- Collects data on pathogenic, zoonotic, and indicator bacteria for the [[Icelandic AMR-AMU Report]]
- Conducts regular **MRSA screenings of pig populations** (2014/2015, 2018, 2020, 2022, 2025)
- Tracks ESBL/AmpC-producing E. coli and multidrug-resistant strains
- Receives antimicrobial sales data from the Icelandic Medicines Agency
- Uses the **Heilsa** central veterinary database
