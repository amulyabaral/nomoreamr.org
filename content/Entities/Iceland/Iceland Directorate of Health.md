# Directorate of Health - Centre for Health Security

- **Country**: [[Iceland]]
- **Type**: Government agency
- **Website**: [landlaeknir.is](https://www.landlaeknir.is/)

The Centre for Health Security and Communicable Disease Control within Iceland's Directorate of Health. The **Chief Epidemiologist** coordinates human [[Antimicrobial Resistance (AMR)|AMR]] and [[Antimicrobial Use (AMU)|AMU]] surveillance.

## Role in AMR/AMU Surveillance

- Collects clinical microbiology data for the [[Icelandic AMR-AMU Report]]
- Coordinates human AMU surveillance
- Hosts the medicines prescription database (outpatient care prescriptions)
- Regulated under the Act on Health Security and Communicable Diseases (No. 19/1997)
