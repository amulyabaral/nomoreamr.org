# Danish Health Data Authority (DHDA)

- **Country**: [[Denmark]]
- **Type**: Government agency
- **Website**: [sundhedsdatastyrelsen.dk](https://sundhedsdatastyrelsen.dk/)

Manages the **Register of Medicinal Product Statistics**, providing data on antibiotic sales and consumption for human use in [[Denmark]].

## Role in AMR/AMU Surveillance

- Maintains the Register of Medicinal Product Statistics
- Provides data on human antibiotic sales and consumption for [[DANMAP]]
- Collaborates with [[SSI]] on [[Antimicrobial Use (AMU)|AMU]] data
