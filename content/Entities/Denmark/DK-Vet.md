# DK-Vet Consortium

- **Country**: [[Denmark]]
- **Type**: Research consortium

A consortium formed by [[SSI|Statens Serum Institut]] and Copenhagen University, contributing to veterinary [[Antimicrobial Resistance (AMR)|AMR]] surveillance in [[Denmark]].

## Role in AMR Surveillance

- Contributes to veterinary AMR surveillance for [[DANMAP]]
- Performs AMR surveillance on clinical isolates from sick food-producing animals and pets
- Works with pathogenic, zoonotic, and indicator bacteria data
