# Danish Veterinary and Food Administration (DVFA)

- **Country**: [[Denmark]]
- **Type**: Government agency
- **Website**: [foedevarestyrelsen.dk](https://www.foedevarestyrelsen.dk/)

Responsible for food safety and animal health in [[Denmark]]. Hosts the **VetStat** national database - one of the most detailed and comprehensive systems for monitoring veterinary drug use worldwide.

## Role in AMR Surveillance

- Collects data on pathogenic, zoonotic, and indicator bacteria for [[DANMAP]]
- Provides data on antibiotic sales and consumption in the veterinary sector

## VetStat Database

VetStat gathers data from:
- Veterinarians
- Pharmacies
- Feed mills that add antimicrobials to feed

It includes information on animal species, age groups, and diagnostic indications - making it one of the most granular veterinary [[Antimicrobial Use (AMU)|AMU]] databases in the world.
