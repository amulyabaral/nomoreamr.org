# DTU Food (National Food Institute)

- **Country**: [[Denmark]]
- **Type**: Research institute
- **Website**: [food.dtu.dk](https://www.food.dtu.dk/)

Part of the Technical University of Denmark. Involved in surveillance of [[Antimicrobial Resistance (AMR)|antimicrobial resistance]] in food and animals, contributing to [[DANMAP]] data on both pathogenic bacteria and antibiotic consumption.

## Role in AMR Surveillance

- Analyses and reports foodborne zoonotic bacteria and indicator bacteria from healthy food-producing animals and meat for [[DANMAP]]
- Provides data on antibiotic sales and consumption in the animal sector
- Analyses VetStat data from [[DVFA]]
