# Norwegian Food Safety Authority (Mattilsynet)

- **Country**: [[Norway]]
- **Type**: Government agency
- **Website**: [mattilsynet.no](https://www.mattilsynet.no/)

Responsible for food safety and animal welfare in [[Norway]]. Coordinates [[NORM-NORM-Vet|NORM-Vet]] and manages the **Veterinary Prescription Register (VetReg)**.

## Role in AMR/AMU Surveillance

- Coordinates [[NORM-NORM-Vet|NORM-Vet]] (implemented by [[NVI]])
- Manages **VetReg** - mandatory reporting for all food-producing animals (including farmed fish) and horses; voluntary for companion animals
- Contributes veterinary antibiotic sales and consumption data
- Wholesalers mandated to report all veterinary medicinal product sales to [[NIPH]]
- Data reported to the European Medicines Agency (EMA)
