# Norwegian Institute of Public Health (NIPH/FHI)

- **Country**: [[Norway]]
- **Type**: Government agency
- **Website**: [fhi.no](https://www.fhi.no/en/)

[[Norway]]'s national public health institute. Central to [[NORM-NORM-Vet|NORM]], providing [[Antimicrobial Resistance (AMR)|AMR]] data from clinical microbiology and managing [[Antimicrobial Use (AMU)|AMU]] databases.

## Role in AMR Surveillance

- Collects clinical microbiology laboratory data for [[NORM-NORM-Vet|NORM]]
- Manages the national notification system for communicable diseases (MSIS)
- High-priority resistance phenotypes submitted to designated reference laboratories

## Role in AMU Surveillance

- Manages the **Norwegian Prescription Database** (all antimicrobials dispensed to outpatients)
- Collects data from the national drug wholesale database
- Provides human antibiotic sales and consumption data
- Contributes to veterinary antibiotic sales data for [[NORM-NORM-Vet|NORM-Vet]]
- Hosts the **WHO Collaborating Center for Drug Statistics Methodology** (ATC/DDD system)
