# Norwegian Veterinary Institute (NVI)

- **Country**: [[Norway]]
- **Type**: Research institute
- **Website**: [vetinst.no](https://www.vetinst.no/en)

[[Norway]]'s national research institute for animal health, fish health, and food safety. Implements [[NORM-NORM-Vet|NORM-Vet]], providing data on [[Antimicrobial Resistance (AMR)|AMR]] in animals.

## Role in AMR Surveillance

- Implements [[NORM-NORM-Vet|NORM-Vet]] (coordinated by [[NFSA]])
- Provides data on pathogenic, zoonotic, and indicator bacteria in animals
- Covers food, feed, and wild animals
- Conducts systematic MRSA screening of pig populations
