# University Hospital of North Norway (UNN)

- **Country**: [[Norway]]
- **Type**: Hospital
- **Website**: [unn.no](https://unn.no/)

Plays a key role in [[NORM-NORM-Vet|NORM]] by coordinating the collection and processing of [[Antimicrobial Resistance (AMR)|AMR]] data from clinical microbiology laboratories across [[Norway]].

## Role in AMR Surveillance

- **Coordinates NORM** from the Department of Microbiology and Infection Control
- Collects and processes AMR data from clinical microbiology laboratories nationwide
- Manages protocol-defined yearly surveys of bacterial and fungal pathogens (running for 25+ years)
