# Swedres-Svarm

**Swedres-Svarm** is [[Sweden]]'s joint surveillance programme combining human (**Swedres**) and veterinary (**Svarm**) [[Antimicrobial Resistance (AMR)|antimicrobial resistance]] monitoring. The results are published in a combined annual report.

- **Swedres established**: 2002
- **Website**: [sva.se](https://www.sva.se/)

## Structure

| Component | Coordinator | Key Partners |
|---|---|---|
| Human AMR (Swedres) | [[PHAS\|Folkhälsomyndigheten]] | Clinical laboratories nationwide |
| Veterinary AMR (Svarm) | [[SVA]] | Slaughterhouses, veterinary clinics |
| AMU data | [[Swedish eHealth Agency]] | [[SVA]], [[SBA]] |

## Human Surveillance (Swedres)

| Component | Data Source |
|---|---|
| Clinical labs | [[PHAS]] |
| Antibiotic sales | [[Swedish eHealth Agency]] |

- Clinical laboratories **automatically report** susceptibility test results into national databases:
  - **SmiNet** - notifiable disease reporting
  - **Svebar** - voluntary AMR data reporting
- Covers both invasive and non-invasive isolates from primary and specialist care
- Routine surveillance for **ESBL-producing E. coli** from urine and bloodstream infections
- Legislative basis: Communicable Diseases Act (Smittskyddslagen, SFS 2004:168)

## Animal & Food Surveillance (Svarm)

| Component | Data Source |
|---|---|
| Pathogenic/zoonotic/indicator bacteria | [[SVA]] |
| Antibiotic sales | [[SVA]], [[SBA]] |

- Managed by [[SVA|Statens Veterinärmedicinska Anstalt]]
- Monitors commensal bacteria in food-producing animals from slaughterhouses
- Routinely collects diagnostic samples from diseased animals

## Environmental Surveillance

- Not yet routine
- Research initiatives underway, including wastewater studies
- See [[Environmental AMR Surveillance]]

## International Reporting

- Human AMR data → **EARS-Net**
- Animal/food AMR data → **EFSA/ECDC**
- Human AMU → **ESAC-Net**
- Veterinary AMU → **ESVAC/ESUAvet**
