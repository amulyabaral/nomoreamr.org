# FINRES / FINRES-Vet

**FINRES** and **FINRES-Vet** are [[Finland]]'s dual surveillance programmes for monitoring [[Antimicrobial Resistance (AMR)|antimicrobial resistance]] in humans and animals, respectively.

- **FINRES**: Human AMR monitoring, coordinated by [[THL]]
- **FINRES-Vet**: Veterinary AMR monitoring, coordinated by [[Finnish Food Authority|Ruokavirasto]]

## Structure

| Component | Coordinator | Key Partners |
|---|---|---|
| Human AMR (FINRES) | [[THL]] | FiRe laboratories network |
| Animal/Food AMR (FINRES-Vet) | [[Finnish Food Authority\|Ruokavirasto]] | [[THL]] |
| AMU data (human + animal) | [[Fimea]] | Pharmaceutical wholesalers |

## Human Surveillance (FINRES)

- Clinical microbiology data from **FiRe laboratories** and THL's mycobacterial laboratory (since 1992)
- [[THL]] compiles, interprets, and publishes annual Finres reports
- **National Infectious Disease Register (NIDR)**: mandatory notification system for 40 infectious diseases and resistant organisms
- Open-access AMR database: [sampo.thl.fi](https://sampo.thl.fi/pivot/prod/fi/finres/lite/fact_lite)
- Grounded in Communicable Diseases Act (2016)

## Animal & Food Surveillance (FINRES-Vet)

| Component | Data Source |
|---|---|
| Pathogenic/zoonotic/indicator bacteria | [[THL]], [[Finnish Food Authority\|Ruokavirasto]] |
| Antibiotic sales | [[Fimea]] |

- Covers industrial food production, meat, fur industry, veterinary clinics for companion animals and horses
- [[Fimea]] provides veterinary antibiotic sales data from pharmaceutical wholesalers

## Environmental Surveillance

- Not yet established as routine surveillance
- See [[Environmental AMR Surveillance]]

## International Reporting

- Human AMR data → **EARS-Net**
- Animal/food AMR data → **EFSA/ECDC**
- Human AMU → **ESAC-Net**
- Veterinary AMU → **ESVAC/ESUAvet**
